T-box Annotation Database

This database contains T-box fold prediction, tRNA pairs from host organisms, information regarding T-box genetic context, and thermodynamic calculations of putative T-box sequences found in nature. Click on individual T-box IDs to access more information. To find a list of searchable parameters, look at our faq page. For advanced search, please use the advanced search feature. The complete database is available to download as a single flat file.
